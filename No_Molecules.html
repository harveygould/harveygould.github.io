Why We Do Not Use Diatomic Molecules


  • We do not fully understand how an electron EDM generates a molecular EDM, how large it would be, and if it would be different in the molecule's rest frame than in the laboratory rest frame.

  • We do not know how to prove the sensitivity of the experiment to an EDM signal.

  • We do not know how to prove that a signal is not an EDM mimic.

  • We would not have sufficient confidence to tell someone proposing a model that our EDM result from a molecule experiment discredited the model, nor the confidence to use the experimental result to help rule out a muon EDM, or to propose an energy scale for a future collider.
