New Electron Electric Dipole Moment Experiments

Using a Fountain of Ultra-Cold Cesium Atoms

Summary

The experimental upper limit to the electron electric dipole moment (EDM) is consequential because it constraints models of Dark Matter, Baryon Asymmetry, Axion-like particles, Supersymmetry, other beyond Standard Model physics. Finding an electron EDM woud be strong evidence of significant new physics.

In addition to sensitivity, a credible electron EDM experiment requires: a rigorous theoretical foundation, a well understood experimental system, tests that verify the experimental sensitivity to an EDM, and tests that set limits to EDM-mimicking effects. Our experiments will meet all of these requirements.
